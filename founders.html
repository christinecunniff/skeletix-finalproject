William Lindsay, Founder, CEO
William C. Lindsay is a recent Cum Laude graduate of Drexel University, who received a degree in Music Industry with a Business Minor and a PreLaw Concentration. As acting COO of Philadelphia based tech startup FeedbackLoop, Lindsay brings both knowledge and experience of the current startup scene. Lindsay is a founder and the creative director for Long Count Artist Management LLC. (W. C. Lindsay, SHMNS), and the producer/vocalist/guitarist of Philadelphia electro-pop act W. C. Lindsay, who were dubbed an "Artist to Start Listening to in 2014" by the Huffington Post. W. C. Lindsay has also received critical acclaim from major media outlets such as Fuse and Redbull Media amongst others, as well as being named one of the "100 Bands You Need To Know in 2014" by Alternative Press. W. C. Lindsay embarked on a nationwide tour in support of their debut album "Easy Victim, Charitable Deceptions" (Big Footprints Records), allowing Lindsay to build and foster an extensive network of musicians, promoters and industry heads across the country.
